Revision

Tips on Revision

Don't get caught up in all of the excitement of university and forget the education part, if you want to make it to 2nd year make sure you put some time aside to revise! Of course it's not the most interesting part of uni but it's defintely essential!

1. The early bird gets the worm

Start way before you'd planned to and do it early in the day, the less time you spend putting it off the longer you will do it for and the more you will learn the less you have to do in the future!

2. The early bird gets the worm

Start way before you'd planned to, the less time you spend putting it off the more you will learn and the less you have to do!

3. Take a break

Your brain will stay focused for longer if you take a short break every now and again, just try to not get distracted!

4. Cut out those distractions

It's easy to get sidetracked so consider finding a silent area in the library so you can really put your head down and work.

5. Try not to look at it as revision!

Most importantly have some fun with it. You will remember things better if you don't feel like your forcing yourself with revision.
