What does a Student Need ?

Lorem Manchester’s nightlife simply speaks for itself. It has become a staple of Manchester’s identity and is something every student needs to experience Assignments stressing you out? Flat mates annoying you? Craving Sambuca? Manchester has you covered and we’ve listed the top 5 clubs to fulfil your alcohol related desires.

1. Bring a pen

You'd be horrified if you knew how many people turned up to their lectures without the most basic equipment. You will more than likely need a pen even if its to sign into the register.

2. Bring a computer

In this day and age almost everything you do will benefit from having the internet as a resource. In addition word processors will make taking notes much easier as images can be added and things can be changed as you learn more.

3. Bring your smartphone

Communication is key. Group projects will require you to keep in touch with people on your course.
