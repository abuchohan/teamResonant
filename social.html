Societies

As a student, you will mostly be searching for cost friendly supermarkets around the campus area, so here are some of the most visited:

  • ALDI (location)

    • A well-known chain around the UK, ALDI is the place where you will get the most for your money. Unfortunately, it is located in the city center which is about a 20-30 minute walk from the campus and you might not want to carry all of your shopping from there.

  • ASDA (location)

    • At ASDA you can find anything for your fridge, whether you want fresh or frozen, fast food or the right spice for a specific dish you can find it there.

  • Sainsbury's (location/s)

    • Sainsbury's has the most shops out of all of them however the variety of products is not that big and the prices are a bit higher because the locations are conveniently placed. They are ideal for small shopping.

  • Local shops

    • Local shops don't tend to be price friendly. They also do not accept card payments from some international banks and prefer cash. However, they are the closest to the accommodations out of all of them.

Tips on revision

Sleeping

Sleeping is necessary in order for us to function normally as human beings. A study has shown that students that get 7-9 hours of sleep at night tend to score higher marks than those who sleep either less or more. It is simply because if you let your brain recover from the tasks given to it in the previous day it will help you concentrate easier, you will lose less focus and you will be able to think of better ideas when it comes to your projects. You will also have more day time which will make you feel more productive in general.
